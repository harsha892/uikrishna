IT Staffing Services by Compvue

Contract Based Services: Under 'Contract Based Services' Compvue's offers contract based staffing solutions to its clients. Also known as 'Project Based Services', contract based solutions are effective for the businesses with fluctuating business needs. It allows businesses to get right technical expertise for their projects. During your project, Compvue helps you with hiring right technical expertise on contract basis. As projects are completed and inventory begins to slow down, Compvue takes care of the discontinuation of the running contracts. From the beginning, Compvue handles all human resource functions and employer related insurance administration.

Contract-to-Hire Services: Under Contract-to Hire Services, Compvue offers flexibility to hire the resource on per-hour basis. The company can contact Compvue to hire a resource for a minimum number of hours. Payroll Staffing Services: Compvue offers Payroll staffing services to the companies. Payroll staffing services include 'payroll generation and distribution', 'payroll tax withholding', 'state and federal reporting requirements' and employer related insure administration.

Open Source

Our Open Source Center of Excellence focuses on many open source technologies in different areas – platforms, databases, development environments, application servers, testing and reporting tools.

The COE strives to standardize the execution and implementation of open source software/products. It is the source of documentation, guidance, shared best practices and metrics for our project teams, developing and maintaining solutions for customers.

Overview

With over 230,000 Open Source applications registered on Sourceforge (in addition to projects on various proprietary exchanges and ISV specific forges), it is often difficult for IT and business executives to select the right applications for their technology needs.

Our Open Source development teams, however, comb the Open Source universe to identify enterprise-ready open source software that fits our business solution criteria. It is the role of this Compvue branch to build its knowledgebase, which involves consolidating fact-based opinion, software interoperability, compatibility, convergence, usability, functionality, and available support.

Our employees strive to standardize the execution and implementation of Open Source software to provide the most efficient and reliable solutions possible.

Primary objectives:

  • Improve legacy and proprietary integration with open source software.
  • Impact usability and architecture decisions among project teams.
  • Increase overall user adoption.
  • Implement the best practices for the development of customers’ solutions.
  • Promote cross-platform flexibility.

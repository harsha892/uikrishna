Electronic Medical Records

Compvue EMR is built based on the practice and workflow as per guidelines of “Health Information Technology”. Our EMR solutions are suitable for small to medium businesses. It automates your practice workflow which minimizes the operational cost and increases the efficiency.

Features of Compvue EMR

  • Capture and store patient information and records effectively
  • Schedule Patient appointments
  • Sends out reminder alerts to users
  • Prepare prescriptions and share them electronically
  • Share the medical records to different entities as per Health Information Technology standards like HL7/DICOM
  • Access and share the records as per HIPPA standard
  • Readily available customized reports and on demand reports
  • Storing the Lab Reports
  • Enhance the application/features based on specific client requirement
  • Backup Database at single click
